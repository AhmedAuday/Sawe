FTTX


FTTH, or Fiber to the Home, is a high-speed broadband network architecture that delivers fiber optic cables directly to residential homes. It provides extremely fast and reliable internet connectivity, enabling high-bandwidth services like video streaming, online gaming, and other data-intensive applications.
